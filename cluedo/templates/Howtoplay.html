What should I do if I want to play?

1. Find people you want to play with

2. Have a pc or laptop for each player

3. Download app, which you can find below

4. Click on play button and create lobby

5. Every player need to write his email and name

App

Game proccess

Any player need to write 'start' in lobby chat, then all players will get a mail with cards. If you want to make an assumption, write 'assumption' in lobby chat. You will get a mail with cards this cards. If you finish you game, write 'finish' in lobby chat.

Have a good game!
